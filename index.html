About CampusGig

CampusGig is a tech-enabled marketplace where customers can book verified college students to help with everyday jobs. Our mission is to provide students with valuable work opportunities while offering reliable, affordable services to the community.

Key Features

Verified Student Workers

Every worker is a current college student, so you know you're supporting education while getting the job done right.

Simple Booking

Easily request services online with transparent pricing and reviews.

Flexible Gigs

Students can choose jobs that match their skills and class schedules.
